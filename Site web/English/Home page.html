Le club de robotique du collège Montmorency

Since 2007, the robotic club welcomes many students, with numerous interest, to participate in the CRC robotic competition and to use their interest to compete in different categories. Our club is located in Montmorency (475 Boulevard de l'Avenir, Laval, QC H7N 5H9).

What is the CRC robotic competition?

CRC is an organization with the objective of making robotic competitions more available to youngsters in high school and college. It also values science, technology, engineering, art and language. The CRC robotic competition is an annual event which asks the participating schools to conceive a robot, program it, working on a program for the programming competition, build a website, assemble a booth and film a presentation video.
